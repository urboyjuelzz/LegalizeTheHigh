Criminal to Commercial Businesses

Many states have already legalized it's recreational use, something of which has lead to the breeding of legitimate businesses built around the service of it's usage.

Through these businesses, the new ways they brand their product(s) and the increasing prevelance of cannabis, we are forced to educate ourselves on the topic and learn the facts. This will only continue to grow and will change the way we view this substance.
